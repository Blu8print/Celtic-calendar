App Description

Roots Calendar is a Celtic Tree Calendar app — 13 months of 28 days, each named after a sacred tree with its own teaching. Experience time differently.

Navigate your days as seasons with purpose: plan events, track the moon, and live in sync with the natural rhythm that guided Celtic peoples for thousands of years. Works fully offline. Your data never leaves your device.
